World-Class Facilities.

A pristine world of comfort and serenity awaits you at Tigris Valley. Every aspect of this splendid retreat is specially curated to make visitors from around the world feel welcome.

Facilities

Experience the glory and freshness of nature in this beautiful valley. Nestled in the lush greenery of God's Own Country, we are fully equipped to host you!

Fitness Centre

An exclusive fitness centre and workout floor with Advanced Fitness programmes.

Rooms

An exceptional nature friendly interior arranged in line with the wellness programme measures.

Healthy Spa & Medicated Massage

Experience fresh skincare routines & rejuvenating therapies that vitalize your skin.

Swimming Pools

Two large and cozy indoor swimming pools to relax and unwind.

Meditation Hall

An extended peaceful area dedicated to meditation programs and yoga, where you can find yourself.

Herbs House

Tigris Valley, in association with Imtibish Herbals concentrates on producing pure, authentic and high end medicines based on traditional science of Ayurveda and Unani.

Lab

We are committed to provide high quality laboratory services relevant to medicine and disease diagnosis to physicians for optimum patient care

Dental Studio

A dental studio which which follows international standards in infrastructure, sterilization, materials and equipments.

Library

Libraries store the energy that fuels the imagination. Come, experience the Library at Tigris Valley that opens up windows to the world .

Board Room

A Meeting room with a fixed setting and a oval table in the middle for a maximum of eighteen people.

Ruhe Cafe

At Ruhe Cafe, we prove that healthy food can be incredibly delicious.

Wellness Restaurant

An exclusive wellness restaurant that helps begin your healthy living journey from the inside.

Choudah Therapy Units

A package of 14 Unani based wellness programs specially planned for your wellbeing & improved health.

Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Centre

A unique treatment center for all kinds of rehabilitation programs.

At Tigris we offer treatment programmes that are tailored to meet the needs of each individual - in terms of nutrition, therapies, and workouts. Each programme is a mix of the AYUSH medicinal philosophies of Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy - giving you well-rounded holistic care that caters to both mind & body.
