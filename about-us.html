About Us

Tigris Valley, located at Markaz Knowledge City, Calicut, Kerala is a holistic healthcare retreat, space away from city chaos, filled with natural care and wellness. We strive to recreate the conventional system of natural health care integrated through traditional treatments in a lush valley below the Western Ghats.

Tigris the Healing Valley

A paradise on earth, Tigris Valley is where your search ends for a complete physical and mental rejuvenation. Liberate yourself from maladies and malaises at this serene haven, blessed with nature’s artistry and professionals’ expertise. Treat yourself to a holistic healing experience provided by experts and enhanced by optimized facilities and infrastructure. Welcome to Tigris Valley, make your respite worthwhile and fruitful.
